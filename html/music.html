Music

I first started making music around late 2018, but I've unfortunately lost most of my earliest creations. The oldest project of mine that I can find is from January 2020; I've lost the title but if I remember correctly I was trying to make something summery. Why a summer song in January? I had attempted to make a Christmas-style song - something of a similar style to an instrumental version of All I Want For Christmas Is You - but I had failed to have it even nearly finished in time for Christmas, so I decided to start as early as possible with my summer music. This ended up being, as far as I can tell, the only summer song I wrote, and I didn't end up listening to it or showing to anyone once that year's summer finally arrived. Oh well.
